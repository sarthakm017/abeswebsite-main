Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA)

Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA) is an initiative of Ministry of Education (MoE), Govt. of India to systematically rank all major higher educational institutions and universities in India on indicators related to “Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development” amongst students and faculties. ARIIA ranking inspires the Indian institutions to reorient their mind-set and build ecosystems to encourage high quality research, innovation and entrepreneurship. ARIIA has focus on quality of innovations and it measures the real impact created by these innovations nationally and internationally.

ABESEC is Categorized as Band ‘C’ institution (rank between 51-75) in the category of private or self-financed colleges/Institutes in Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Ranking (ARIIA-2020) announced on 18th August, 2020.
