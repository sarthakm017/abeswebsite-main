The work of an engineer is to learn technology and use technology with intellect, for the comfort and benefit of mankind. Engineers are expected to conceptualize. Cipher, design, create, analyze and solve complex problems. Engineers form the backbone of any country’s economy and hence engineers are to be skilled, made employable, and are to be trained with the skills of management, teamwork, leadership, emerging technologies, critical thinking ability, and soft skills.At the global level, engineering education is experiencing a paradigm shift from content based education to outcome based education. Industries often complain about the non-availability of employable engineers to fulfill their organizational needs of trained technical human resources.We at ABESEC aim to bridge the gap and meet the demand between industry and academia by training, skilling and re-skilling our students to meet the challenges of the industries. The various training programs offered by the departments provide confidence to our young engineers, build professional attitude and develop the skill-set so as our students are employable from day one, in the organizations; where they are placed. Our trainings help our students to face the challenges of the interview processes and that of the workplace; to be passionate about their trade and to be an asset for their organization.
